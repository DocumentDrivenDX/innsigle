# Mark research — craft seal inspiration

Inputs for the Innsigle stamp face. Steal structure, not costumes.

## Traditions

| Tradition | What it is | Steal | Leave |
|-----------|------------|-------|-------|
| **Ancient stamp seals** (Near East, earlier than cylinders) | Carved matrix pressed into clay; geometric then figurative | Closed **field** + rim; design as **impression** of a matrix; durable identity | Narrative scenes, cylinder rolls |
| **Cylinder seals** | Rolled intaglio on clay | “One object, many impressions”; identity bound to a physical matrix | Continuous friezes as logo |
| **Norse / Viking-age craftsman marks** | Workshop stamps on metalwork; geometric/runic stamps in archaeology | **Angular geometry**, proprietary stamp as maker voice | Fantasy runes as product cosplay; copying real historic house marks |
| **Old English / medieval craft marks** | Goldsmith & pewter **touch marks**; guild punches; merchant marks | Simple unique glyph in a **bounded field**; multiple punches for different claims | Full armorial clutter |
| **Hallmarks** (later English/European silver) | Stack of small punches (maker, assay, date…) | **Secondary countermark** for state (H/M/A) separate from main matrix | Traffic-light morals as “assay” |
| **Pottery potter’s marks** | Impressed or underglaze stamps, cartouches, monograms | Cartouche / lozenge inside a round stamp; deep impression readability | Factory transferware logos |
| **Livestock / iron brands** | Hot iron proprietary glyph | High-contrast **solid silhouette** that reads at distance | Cruelty metaphors in copy |

## Design rules derived

1. **Matrix, not chrome** — solid disk is the wax/clay face; light lines are raised impression.
2. **Field + rim** — outer bead or double rim (seal matrix / guild touch), not a single hairline.
3. **One proprietary monogram** — angular stave + thryst + lozenge; unique to Innsigle; not a letter logo only.
4. **Countermark for composition** — H / M / A as a small second punch (hallmark stack), same family.
5. **Readable small** — silhouette survives at ~24–48px; no fine filigree dependence.
6. **No purity color coding** — monochrome stamp; state is shape/letter, not green/red.

## Product mapping

| Historical idea | Innsigle |
|-----------------|----------|
| Seal matrix | House/person key + claim (who can impress) |
| Impression on clay | Visible mark on a page/post |
| Colophon of production | Composition + ingredients (the colo) |
| Hallmark stack | Optional composition countermark on the face |

## Out of scope for the glyph

- Literal Vegvísir / ægishjálmr / trademarked neopagan symbols  
- Detector shield / green check “verified true”  
- Separate human-only purity brand
