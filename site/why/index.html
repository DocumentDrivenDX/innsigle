Why Innsigle

This page is for evaluators: what problem Innsigle attacks, and why existing layers do not cover dual-channel makers.

The gap

LayerExamplesMiss
Craft badgeNot By AIHuman-only; no proud AI bill; no crypto
Media provenanceC2PA Content CredentialsFile/media-first; social strips metadata
Vendor watermarkSynthIDSparse signal; not a maker BOM
Platform labelsMeta “Made with AI”Not portable; not your brand
DetectorsClassifiersWrong job; arms race

Thesis

One seal family for two real jobs:

  1. UC-AI-docs — Owned docs with a signed colophon that can name models proudly.
  2. UC-human-social — Human-authored mark that survives metadata death.

Editorial tools such as sloptimizer clean prose; they do not rewrite composition to human-authored. List them on the bill.
